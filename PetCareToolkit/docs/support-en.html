How do I add a new pet?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen, then fill in your pet's information including name, species, breed, and other details.
How does nutrition calculation work?
Our nutrition calculator uses veterinary-approved formulas based on your pet's weight, age, activity level, and life stage to calculate daily calorie requirements.
Is my data synced across devices?
Yes! With iCloud sync enabled, your pet data is automatically synchronized across all your iOS devices signed in with the same Apple ID.
What premium features are available?
Premium features include unlimited pets, professional health reports, advanced analytics, weight trend charts, and priority support.
How do I restore my premium purchase?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Premium Features ‚Üí Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
