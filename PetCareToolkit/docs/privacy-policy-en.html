Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 18, 2025

Last Updated: August 18, 2025

Summary: PetCare Toolkit is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. All your pet data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced through your personal iCloud account.

1. Information We Collect

PetCare Toolkit collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Pet Information: Name, breed, age, weight, and other basic pet details you provide
  • Health Records: Vaccination records, medical examinations, medication records, and health notes
  • Weight Records: Weight tracking data and measurements
  • App Preferences: Language settings, units (metric/imperial), and app configuration

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide pet care management features within the app
  • Calculate nutrition recommendations and medication dosages
  • Generate health reports and tracking charts
  • Send you reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is primarily stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, data is synchronized through Apple's CloudKit service using your personal iCloud account
  • No Third-Party Servers: We do not store your data on any third-party servers or cloud services
  • Encryption: Data is protected using Apple's built-in security and encryption protocols

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

  • No advertising networks or analytics services have access to your data
  • No third-party integrations that could access your pet information
  • Your data remains private and under your complete control

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data directly within the app
  • Modify: Edit or update any information at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual records or all data through the app
  • Export: Data can be exported or shared as needed
  • iCloud Control: Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time

6. Third-Party Services

PetCare Toolkit uses the following Apple services:

  • CloudKit: For optional iCloud synchronization (controlled by you)
  • Local Notifications: For reminders and alerts (if permitted)
  • Camera/Photo Library: For pet photos (if permitted)

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

PetCare Toolkit does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for pet owners and caregivers of all ages, but no age-specific data is collected.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or this webpage. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:
