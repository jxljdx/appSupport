About MarkSnap

MarkSnap is a powerful screenshot annotation tool designed for professionals who need to create clear, precise visual communications. Whether you're creating tutorials, reporting bugs, or collaborating with team members, MarkSnap provides all the tools you need.

Key Features

  • Intuitive annotation tools (arrows, text, shapes, highlights)
  • Precise editing capabilities
  • High-quality export options
  • Clean, user-friendly interface
  • Quick sharing functionality

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

If you've purchased Premium on this device before, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your Premium features without additional charge.

Can I use MarkSnap on multiple devices?

Yes! Your Premium purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device using the same Apple ID.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through your Apple ID account or contact Apple Support directly.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. MarkSnap:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track user behavior
  • Processes images locally on your device
  • Does not upload images to external servers
  • Only uses necessary device permissions
